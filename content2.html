Exercise during quarantine
  • Admin Mayfit
  • AUG 30 27, 2021
  • 4 Comments

Planks are exercises that build balance and core muscles. It is one of the asanas, or vinyasa, in yoga. You can also think of it as a separate exercise. There are two main types of plank: the full plank, which is balanced with both arms, and the side plank, which is balanced with one arm. Best of all, you can do planks at home with just a yoga mat.

  1. Do push-ups on a yoga mat. This is the easiest way to get into plank.


  2. Slowly bend your forearms down until your elbows and fists touch the floor. Make sure your palms are clenched and placed directly under your shoulders


  3. Curl your toes down, lean back your sitting bones, and push your belly button inward toward your spine to work your abdominal muscles.


  4. Keep your body straight and your neck and spine in a neutral position.


  5. Tighten your abs and squeeze your glutes. These two major muscle groups are the main exercise object of this exercise.


  6. Hold this plank position until burning sensation occurs. Look ahead to the ground. Avoid arching your back and keep your body flat from the back of your feet to the back of your head.
